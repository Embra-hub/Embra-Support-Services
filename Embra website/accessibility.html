Accessibility Statement

Embra Support Services is committed to ensuring our website is accessible to all users, including people with disabilities. We aim to meet recognized accessibility standards and continuously improve the user experience.

Our Efforts

  • Using clear, simple language
  • Maintaining good color contrast
  • Providing alt text for images
  • Ensuring keyboard navigability

Limitations

While we strive to make our site fully accessible, some content may not yet meet all standards. We are working to update and improve these areas.
