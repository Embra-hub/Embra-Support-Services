Embra Support Services is a compassionate homecare provider that supports elderly individuals in the comfort of their homes.

We offer non-medical, person-centered services to promote independence and wellbeing, led by qualified professionals including Registered Nurses.

Disclaimer

Embra Support Services offers non-medical, in-home support led by qualified nurses, focused on health and wellbeing. We are not an emergency service. Please dial 999 or 112 in urgent situations. Services such as check-ins, pillbox setups, and reminders are supportive only — not clinical or diagnostic. Clients remain responsible for providing accurate healthcare information and taking medications as directed. We do not administer injection treatments unless specially arranged in advance. Services may be adjusted or declined if considered unsafe or beyond our scope.
